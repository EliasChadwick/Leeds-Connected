Dynamic Business Services are one of the UK's leading E-learning design companies with over 20 years of experience. Their aim is to deliver high-impact online learning for any audience making sure to provide efficiency, knowledge, productivity and profitability throughout your organisation.

Dynamic offers a wide range of creative services including virtual tours, XR development and even game design. One piece of work they have produced recently was creating an interactive game in collaboration with Scottish Water, which would later be published to primary school children throughout Scotland. Children would go on an interactive journey where they discover the consequences to the environment when the wrong things are flushed down the toilet or poured down the sink and therefore discover what they can do to help.

Dynamic also offer a very successful student placement scheme with 80% of placement students going on to be full-time employees at the business. 6 students were employed from end of year shows last year.

Along with Scottish Water, Dynamic has worked with the likes of Sainsbury's, Next and the NHS.
