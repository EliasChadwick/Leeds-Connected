For more than ten years Umpf has been providing clients with high-impact, creative PR, digital and social media campaigns. Engaging work that delivers results for regional, national and global businesses. The designers in our Studio add the creative pizzazz. And our paid team are bang on the money.

Umpf are very well known for their PR stunts and won multiple awards within this sector of the creative industry. One well known stunt was "Up Yours I quit". Created in partnership with Multilotto, Umpf created PR for the statistic stating that 51% of Brits had dreamt of quitting their job if they won the lottery. Their stunt involved creating a resignation letter spray painted onto a car and then parking the car in a busy area of Leeds city centre. The stunt was a success and created a 600% increase in hits to multilotto.co.uk.

As well as Multilotto. Umpf has also worked with the likes of Trinity Leeds, Lidl and Jet2 Holidays.

Some of Umpf's awards consist of Integrated Agency of the Year 2015-18 PR Stunt of the Year 2018-20.
